Thuli plaukai trumpi.

Thuli has short hair.

Anos plaukai ilgi.

Anna has long hair.

Ketės plaukai ilgiausi.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Zama susipynė plaukus.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Baba yra su barzda.

Baba has a beard.

Zanelė susišukavo plaukus.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Thabo apsikirpo.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Themba nusiskuto plaukus.

Themba has shaved his hair.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Lina Baltrukoniene
