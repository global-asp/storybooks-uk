Mira a los animales.

Look at the animals.

La vaca dice, “Muu.”

The cow says, “Moo.”

La cabra dice, “Be, be.”

The goat says, “Meh, meh.”

El caballo dice, “Hin.”

The horse says, “Neigh.”

El cerdo dice, “Oink-oink.”

The pig says, “Grunt.”

La gallina dice, “Cló-cló.”

The chicken says, “Cluck.”

El perro dice, “Guau.”

The dog says, “Woof.”

El granjero dice, “¡Chsss!”

The farmer says, “Shhh!”

Written by: Jenny Katz
Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Karina Vásquez
Read by: Áurea Vericat
Language: Spanish
Level: Level 1
