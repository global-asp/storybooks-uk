Weather book Weather book Le livre de la météo

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Sandra McDougall

Darshan Soni

English

Level 1

It is dry.

It is dry.

Il fait sec.

The sun is hot.

The sun is hot.

Le soleil est chaud.

It is windy.

It is windy.

Il fait du vent.

It is cloudy.

It is cloudy.

Il y a des nuages.

It is cold.

It is cold.

Il fait froid.

It is raining.

It is raining.

Il pleut.

There is thunder.

There is thunder.

Il y a du tonnerre.

I see a rainbow.

I see a rainbow.

Je vois un arc-en-ciel.
