Look at the animals.

Look at the animals.

Regardez les animaux.

The cow says, "Moo."

The cow says, "Moo."

La vache dit, « Meuh. »

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

La chèvre dit, « Bêê, bêê. »

The horse says, "Neigh."

The horse says, "Neigh."

Le cheval dit, « Hiiii. »

The pig says, "Grunt."

The pig says, "Grunt."

Le cochon dit, « Groin, groin. »

The chicken says, "Cluck."

The chicken says, "Cluck."

La poule dit, « Cot-cot. »

The dog says, "Woof."

The dog says, "Woof."

Le chien dit, « Wouf. »

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

Le fermier dit, « Chut ! »
